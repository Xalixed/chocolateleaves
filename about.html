Things you should know

Stuff about the system as a whole that we think is important enough to put here. We may be vague or cut some things out for the sake of brevity, so please don't be afraid to ask about anything here!

  • We are not diagnosed. We currently have no viable path to get a proper diagnosis for anything due to our current IRL situation. We just feel more comfortable with self-diagnosing rather than trying to stay as one identity until we can actually talk to a professional, which will likely be in a couple years.
    Please don't fakeclaim without showing evidence, though. We've done our own research.
  • We think we have OSDD-1B, which is similar to DID, but with no/less amnesia between alters. Please don't tell one of us something you wouldn't want another to know!
  • It can feel vague as to who's fronting if we aren't already talking to someone. If you want to start a conversation with us, just talk to us normally at first! We'll both figure out who's fronting from there.
  • IN REAL LIFE we prefer to just go by one name and one set of pronouns that everyone's comfortable with, since the system stuff is 300% messier IRL than it is online, and we'd rather keep things simple. If you know us IRL, feel free to ask us!
